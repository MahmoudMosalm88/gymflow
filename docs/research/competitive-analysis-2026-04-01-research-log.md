### Result
Filled.

### New knowledge from Round 2
- Users care most about workflow quality, support quality, and operational simplicity.
- Missing-feature complaints cluster around staff mobile workflows, reporting, booking logic, messaging, and customization.
- Pricing is less about one universal number and more about the divide between self-serve transparent pricing and quote-led enterprise pricing.
- Competitor messaging is heavily commoditized at the top level; the second clause is what differentiates them.
- Keyword demand exists, but much of the opportunity is long-tail and comparison-driven.
- Best channels are SEO/local SEO, Google Ads, referrals, reviews/GBP, and retention automation.
- Product Hunt is an optional amplifier, not a core acquisition path for GymFlow.
- The category is shifting toward AI, wearables, mobile self-service, community retention, and payments recovery.
